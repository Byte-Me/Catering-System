To prepare

This is a list containing the orders to prepare. The order status can be updated quickly and easily from the checkbox in the rightmost column of each order. The first time the checkbox is clicked, the status updates from "to prepare" to "in progress", one more click updates the status to "ready for delivery", and the order is removed from the to prepare section.
