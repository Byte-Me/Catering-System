Chef Help Page

What does the left-list show?

This is a list of upcoming orders that has to be made on that day.

What does the right-list show?

This is a list of your storage. This shows which ingredients you got in store and how much of it you have.

How do I add a new ingredient to the storage list?

Click on “New Ingredient” on the top-right. The list on the top shows what ingredients are in storage. On the bottom of the window you can fill in the information needed to add a new ingredient. Afterwards you click on “Add”. When you have added everything you want, you click “Add Ingredient(s)” to add it to storage.

How do I edit an ingredient?

You need to mark the ingredient you want to edit in the list on the right and click “Edit Ingredient”. Then you change what you want to change and click “Edit Ingredient”. If you do not want to change anything you can click “Cancel”.

How do I generate a shopping list, and how does it work?

Click the “Generate Shopping List” button on the top-right of the window. Change the dates by clicking on the “…”-icons next to the dates. Then you click “Update List”. A shopping list will appear showing the ingredients needed to make the orders that is in the database for time-period you chose. You need to note down which ingredients you need to buy and then check the “Add ingredients to storage” box before clicking “Ok”. Now the ingredients from the shopping list will appear in the storage list.

How do I make new recipes?

Click the “Recipes” button on the top-left. Then you click the “Add Recipe” button. A list of ingredients will appear and you need to choose what you need for your recipe. Double click or mark an ingredient and then click the left arrow to drag it to the ingredients-in-recipe list. Write down how many units of the ingredient you want in the recipe. When you have added all the ingredients needed, you click “Add Recipe”. Write down the name of the recipe and the price afterwards.

How do I show or edit a recipe?

Click the “Recipes” button on the top-left. Mark the recipe you want to show/edit. Then you click the “Show/Edit Recipe” button. You can now add new ingredients to the recipe by double clicking or mark an ingredient and then click the left arrow. You need to write how many units of that ingredient you want to add to the recipe. If you want to remove an ingredient from the recipe you can mark it in the ingredients-in-recipe list and click the right arrow. When you are finished editing, you click “Edit Recipe” and choose a new recipe price. If you do not want to change anything, you can click “Cancel”.

Which user types have access to the chef panel?

Admin- and chef users have access to this panel.
