Customers Help Page

How do I add a new customer?

By clicking on the “New Customer” button on the top-right. You can choose if you want the customer to be a “Private” or a “Corporation” user by clicking on the one you want. Afterwards you fill in the information and click “Add Customer”.

How do I show or edit a user?

You can double click on the user you want to show/edit or you can mark the user and click the “Show/Edit Customer” button on the top-right. Here you can change the details you want about a customer and finish the process by clicking “Edit Customer”. If you do not want any changes, you can click “Cancel”.

How do I delete customers?

You can mark the customer you want to delete and click “Delete Customer”.

How do I reactivate old users?

Go to the list below and find the customer you want to reactivate. Mark this customer and click “Reactivate Customer” on the right side of the window.

How do I search for customers?

Write what you want to find in the “Search Customers”-bar to search through the customer-database.

Which user types have access to the customers panel?

Admin- and sale users have access to this panel.
