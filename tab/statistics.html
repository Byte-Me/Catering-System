Statistics Help Page

How do I change the statistic-time frame?

By clicking on the “…”-icons next to the dates on the top of the window, you can choose a from-date and a to-date. Afterwards you click “Get Statistics”.

What does the top-chart show?

The top chart shows the amount orders per time-unit. It will show per day, per week or per month, depending on how long time you choose.

What does the window on the bottom-left show?

The window on the left shows some general, useful statistics from the given time-period.

What does the bottom-right-chart show?

The chart on the right shows the amount of orders on different weekdays in the time-period.

Which user types have access to the statistics panel?

Admin- and sale users have access to this panel.
