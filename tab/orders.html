Orders Help Page

How do I make a new order?

Firstly, you need to click the “New Order” button on the top-right. Choose which customer the order applies to, by clicking on the “Customer” drop-down menu. Then you need to choose a date by clicking on the “…”-icon next to the date. Choose time of the day in the “Time”-bar on the top-right. Now you need to add the recipes. You do this by marking the recipes in the window on the right side. You can search the recipes in the search bar over the window. You can double click the recipe or mark it and then click the left arrow to drag it over to the order information. A window will automatically pop up and you choose how many portions of the chosen recipe you want to add. You can comment below the recipe-list. Click “Add Order” to finish the process.

How do I show or edit an order?

You can double click on the order you want to show/edit or you can mark the order and click the “Show/Edit Order” button on the top-right. Here you can change the details you want about an order and finish the process by clicking “Edit Order”. If you do not want any changes, you can click “Cancel”.

How do I delete an order?

You can mark the order you want to delete and click “Delete Order”.

How do I search for orders?

Write what you want to find in the “Search Order”-bar to search through the order-database.

Which user types have access to the orders panel?

Admin- and sale users have access to this panel.
